Tarot Card Meanings by Topic

Tarot cards represent symbolic themes that often relate to different areas of life such as relationships, personal direction, emotional awareness, and important decisions.

If you are exploring tarot with a particular theme in mind, the guide below highlights cards that frequently appear in readings connected to those topics.

  • Two of Cups
  • Six of Cups
  • The Empress
  • Ace of Cups

Tarot Cards for Career & Direction

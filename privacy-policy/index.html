Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Symboliclarity handles information when you use this website. We aim to be transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We may use analytics services to understand how visitors use our site. This can include standard data such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and approximate location. This information helps us improve the site and understand usage patterns. We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it — for example, when you use our contact form or send us an email.

Our Yes/No Oracle and Three-Card Tarot tools do not send your questions or inputs to our servers. Responses are generated without storing your input. Your reflections stay in your browser.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies for site function. If we use third-party services — such as analytics or advertising networks — those providers may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Ad Networks

This site may display advertising, including through services such as Google AdSense. Ad networks may collect data about your visits to this and other sites to provide relevant ads. They have their own privacy policies governing that data. We do not control how ad networks use the information they collect. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and opt-out options at the Google Privacy & Terms site.

No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with third parties for their marketing purposes. Any data we collect is used to operate and improve this site.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@symboliclarity.com. We may update this policy from time to time; the current version will be available on this page.
